Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, ICERT, was held on August 19 - 21, 2018, in Madrid, Spain. ICERT is one of the 4 conferences that make up the World Congress on New Technologies (NewTech'18). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.
